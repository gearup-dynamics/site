FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you service all vehicle makes and models?

Yes, we service domestic and import communter vehicles and light-duty trucks. We also service diesel vehicles and small engines.

Do I need to supply parts?

No, we source parts and quote them before any work begins. You are welcome to supply your own parts without any parts or workmanship guarantees or warranties by our team.

What is your service area?

We service anywhere within Calgary, Okotoks, Chestermere, and Airdrie city limits. Feel free to contact us if you are unsure. A mobile service fee may apply outside of Calgary.

How do I pay?

We accept credit, debit, and e-Transfer. Cash payments are accepted without change as we do not carry cash on hand.
